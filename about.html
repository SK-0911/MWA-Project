About Us

The Placements & Corporate Engagements at SVKM'S NMIMS Navi Mumbai consists of the Placement Committee and Corporate Relations involved in facilitating Summer Placements & Final Placements. Being the corporate interface of the college, the placement committee ensures the readiness of the students for the corporate world and aspires to build a strong Corporate-Academia relationship as to skill-up our students by encouraging them to participate in various inter college-corporate events, live projects, academic competitions, and industry workshops to nurture and develop them into business managers. The placement committee is a team of highly motivated individuals who enthusiastically work for the overall institutional goal of student uplifting and inviting reputed organizations and providing desired placement offers to students based on their dreams and aspirations. We pride in ourselves to be an ethical and transparent body and work relentlessly to make our placement process seamless, engaging and rewarding for all parties involved.
